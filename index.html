How to Use ExcelSend

Who we are...

Thanks to our decades of experience managing business critical messaging for small businesses like yours, we have messaging in our DNA. Through continuous innovation alongside an evolving world, we now provide a modern, durable, and scalable service that allows businesses to engage with their customers via email, voice, and SMS messaging using data from Microsoft Excel spreadsheets.

What separates us from everyone else? Our decades of experience have helped us discover the perfect formula to help you engage with your client base while reducing costs and saving time! We’re so good at what we do that our services are relied on by hospitals, government agencies, postal services, and countless private businesses like yours.

We live in an age of constant communication, meaning businesses are constantly competing for the attention of their clients and customers. That’s become a problem for small businesses who constantly suffer from clients and customers missing appointments and reservations. Time, money, and resources are all wasted…

With ExcelSend, that problem is solved! Thanks to its innovative design, ExcelSend uses the contact information in your spreadsheets to send the messages you want precisely when you want in a personalized, engaging, and automated way.

ExcelSend gives you the power to:

Automatically send personalized SMS text alerts to your clients using your spreadsheet data!

• Remind clients of upcoming appointments and reservations.
• Alert clients about an overdue balance, price change, etc.

Have two-way conversations with your clients!

• You’ll receive your clients’ responses via email and can then reply back via email.

Send highly customized and adaptable messages to your clients!

• Access the information you’ve already stored in order to send detailed messages regarding dates, times, dollar amounts, etc.

Keep your data safe and secure!

• With ExcelSend for Windows 10, your data stays on your network.
• All messages are prepared locally and can be individually previewed.

Customize and personalize every message!

• Whether you’re sending to five, 500,000, or more, each message can be individually scheduled and personalized.

Avoid software requirements using the ExcelSend Web Edition on any device!
