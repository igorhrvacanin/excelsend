Setting Up (Desktop Edition)

You’ll need an account with DiaryBook to get started with ExcelSend. DON’T WORRY! We can get you registered and signed up in no time! Once you have ExcelSend, follow the instructions below to get your Desktop Edition set up:

1. Open ExcelSend.
2. Click on ‘Settings.’
3. In the ‘Settings’ window click ‘Open DiaryBook.’
4. Your browser will open. After you sign in or register with DiaryBook the dashboard will show you your Access Key. Copy the Access Key, go back to the ‘Settings’ window, and paste it in the box
5. Click ‘Save.’

Reply numbers

Every business has an identity, and every identity should properly portray who and what that business is. With that in mind, consider that every message must come from somewhere. In the case of ExcelSend, that means the messages you send to your clients and customers will include a reply number. Without a suitable reply number, an ExcelSend service number will be used in place of your reply number on the message sent through ExcelSend. This has the potential to cause issues because the reply number seen by your clients or customers will likely be from a different state and area code. Obviously, this isn’t an ideal setup for your business. However, you can purchase a reply number that’s most suitable for your business - one that includes your local area code, for example. So, all the messages you send through ExcelSend will originate from that number, giving your clients or customers peace of mind. Any replies from your clients or customers will be sent to an email address you provide. A small monthly fee is incurred for reply numbers.
